About Me:

Let me tell you a bit about myself, before we move on to my previous work.

I'm a 30 year old fellow, currently on my 3rd semester as a Multimediadesigner at KEA. Before i started at KEA, i've had a taste of alot of educations - meaning my skills are broad, ranging from woodwork to coding to rehabilitation of wild animals.

When i haven't been working or studying, i've been travelling, and volunteered in South Africa and Thailand on nature conservation projects with Projects Abroad. Here i've learned how to stand on my own feet in a completely different culture, while being almost completely isolated from the "real" world.

My passion has always been with design and aesthetics - no matter the subject. Everything started with a camera, and a love for cinematography, both still and moving.
Now as a Multimedia Designer, my creative life is oriented around branding, content creation and web-design as a whole. I love challenging the conventions by thinking out of the box and provoke the ideas of functional design.
