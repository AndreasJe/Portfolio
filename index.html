About Me:

Let me tell you a bit about myself, before we move on to my previous work.

I'm a 30 year old fellow, currently on my 3rd semester as a Multimediadesigner at KEA. Before i started at KEA, i've had a taste of alot of educations - meaning my skills are broad, ranging from woodwork to coding to rehabilitation of wild animals.

When i haven't been working or studying, i've been travelling, and volunteered in South Africa and Thailand on nature conservation projects with Projects Abroad. Here i've learned how to stand on my own feet in a completely different culture, while being almost completely isolated from the "real" world.

My passion has always been with design and aesthetics - no matter the subject. Everything started with a camera, and a love for cinematography, both still and moving.
Now as a Multimedia Designer, my creative life is oriented around branding, content creation and web-design as a whole. I love challenging the conventions by thinking out of the box and provoke the ideas of functional design.

When we had some knowledge of HTML and CSS, we were handed the assignment to create our own game. With the lack of skills, we were taught Javascript from day one, and had to put together our own software. Starting at the State Diagram, i build this idea, and made the characters, background and settings. As a Javascript rookie i gaped a bit too high, and did end up getting some errors on the final day.

In a group of four, we had to make a game, like we previously did ourselves. As an assigment, we had to create a game involving children of the age of 12-17.
Here we had to work together in Github and do destinctive versioning across the development. I created most of the characters and backgrounds, while helping along with the code, where i could.

This website was our first real project with a real business. In a group of four, we had to produce a video, content and a new website - a total redesign of the business. Everything went well to begin with, but as the coding began, i was left on my own. So here we have my first site, build entirely on Flexbox. - Click the thumbnail to open the site.

We had a bit too much fun with the concept, as you can probably tell, but i did manage to put together a proper website. Not only is it butter smooth, but its my first time using Bootstrap 5. I'm also happy with the packaging and concept overall. - Click the thumbnail to open the site.
